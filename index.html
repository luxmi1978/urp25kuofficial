About Us

URP-25

URP-25 is the 35th batch of students enrolled in the Urban and Rural Planning Discipline under the School of Science, Engineering and Technology (SET) at Khulna University, one of Bangladesh’s leading institutions for higher education in planning and design. Our academic journey began in 2025, but our collective mission and identity are already taking shape.
We are a group of 40 students — each with our own background, strengths, and stories — brought together by a shared commitment to shaping better spaces for people and the planet. We come from different corners of the country, bringing diverse perspectives and ideas to the table. What unites us is a passion for planning — not just as a profession, but as a responsibility to society.
Our studies cover a wide spectrum of disciplines — from urban design, transportation planning, and GIS to environmental planning, housing, and community development. But more than just theory, we are learning how to listen, engage, and co-create with the people whose lives our plans will touch.
This website is our digital home — a space to document our growth, showcase our work, and celebrate our milestones. It is a reflection of our identity as the URP-25 family — creative, collaborative, and committed to making a difference.

Our Mission

To build a community of socially responsible and forward-thinking planners who are equipped to address the spatial, social, and environmental challenges of today and tomorrow.
We aim to blend innovation, critical thinking, and compassion in our approach to planning — ensuring that the spaces we design are not just efficient, but equitable and meaningful.

Our Vision

To be catalysts for sustainable change —
Creating cities that are smarter and more inclusive, rural areas that are resilient and vibrant, and communities that are empowered and connected.
We envision a future where planning is not just about structures, but about people, purpose, and possibility.

Why URP?

Urban and Rural Planning is more than just shaping cities or mapping rural spaces — it's about shaping lives. We chose URP because it stands at the intersection of vision and reality, where big ideas meet everyday needs. It’s where creativity blends with logic, where we don’t just dream of better communities — we build them. To us, URP means responsibility and hope. It means having the power to design spaces that are inclusive, sustainable, and resilient. It's about listening to the land, understanding the people, and weaving both into a future that works for everyone. URP gives us a purpose — to solve real-world challenges, to reimagine what's possible, and to leave behind a legacy of thoughtful planning and meaningful change. Because planning isn’t just about places — it’s about people.

First Impressions of KU

A university in the heart of nature — calm, green, and full of promise.
Khulna University welcomed us with more than just its scenic beauty. From the thoughtful architecture to the peaceful surroundings, every detail reflects a space built for learning and growth. Here, silence is not emptiness — it’s a quiet strength that inspires focus and reflection. What sets KU apart is its freedom — no student politics, no session delays. Time moves forward here, just as we do. It’s a place where education flows without interruption, where unity matters more than division. In this serene environment and welcoming community, we’ve found the perfect beginning — not just for academic life, but for becoming the best version of ourselves.
